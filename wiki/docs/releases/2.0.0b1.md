# CaeReflex 2.0.0b1

`2.0.0b1` begins the Gate 6 beta line. ReflexCase schema remains `1.0`, the backend-neutral inspection contract remains `2.0-alpha.3`, and the independently persisted spatial graph format is `1.0`.

## Included

- Canonical geometry, mesh, grouping and dataset entity contracts.
- Canonical spatial relation vocabulary and directionality.
- Coordinate frames with explicit origin, basis, dimension, handedness, units, parent transforms, confidence and review state.
- Explicit unresolved and conflicted frame states.
- Compact framed bounds.
- ArrayRef-only links for coordinates, connectivity, memberships, fields and transforms.
- SQLite graph persistence with graph-scoped foreign keys.
- Transactional snapshot import and deterministic export.
- Compact spatial graph references in ReflexCase metadata.

## Critical interpretation rule

CaeReflex does not assume metres, a zero origin, global Cartesian axes or right-handed orientation when the source does not provide that evidence. Unknown remains unknown.

## Not included

This release does not map OpenFOAM, Gmsh or VTK outputs into the graph. It does not infer adjacency, transform coordinates, establish cross-format equivalence, run physics rules or maintain human review history.

## Compatibility

Existing ReflexCase schema-v1 payloads remain valid. Gate 5 native inspection and lazy-array contracts are unchanged. Spatial graphs live outside ReflexCase and are referenced through additive metadata.

## Safety

CaeReflex does not validate simulations, prove geometric correctness, assess mesh adequacy, certify coordinate units, establish physical validity or determine design safety.
