01

Raw simulation files are not AI context.

Physics-AI needs an evidence layer between heterogeneous engineering artefacts and model reasoning.

Simulation intelligence is trapped across solver conventions, mesh topology, binary-heavy arrays, coordinate frames, field associations, scripts, revisions, and expert memory. CaeReflex turns that fragmented estate into governed evidence.

05

Why this layer is difficult to reproduce.

The defensibility lies in the interaction of format depth, evidence semantics, physics protocols, and lifecycle governance.

01

Native format depth

Engineering formats encode topology, fields, time, dictionaries, associations, and partial evidence differently. CaeReflex preserves those distinctions instead of asserting false equivalence.

02

Evidence-state semantics

Decoded, inferred, user-supplied, conflicted, missing, and unavailable evidence remain explicit throughout the system.

03

Heavy-data separation

Large coordinates, connectivity, and fields stay behind verified, content-addressed, bounded handles rather than inflating model prompts.

04

Spatial abstraction

Backend-specific geometry and topology map into common entities, coordinate frames, relations, bounds, and array links.

05

Deterministic physics protocol

Versioned rules expose applicability, required evidence, assumptions, limitations, remediation, and stable outcomes.

06

Governed operational history

Immutable snapshots, exact-path temporal diffs, restricted transitions, and append-only reviews connect decisions to evidence over time.

06

Maturity that can be inspected.

CaeReflex does not ask visitors to trust marketing claims; the implementation, contracts, tests, and release controls are visible.

Current technical proof

Versioned, bounded, and acceptance-gated

  • Native OpenFOAM, Gmsh, and VTK inspection paths with malformed-input and fallback tests.
  • Backend-neutral spatial contracts, coordinate frames, relations, queries, and acceptance freeze.
  • Deterministic physics-consistency protocol and versioned OpenFOAM CFD rule pack.
  • Immutable projects and revisions, temporal comparison, human review, jobs, and bounded REST services.
  • Python 3.10–3.12 support, local-first operation, read-only source policy, and explicit safety boundaries.
Platform properties

Designed for controlled integration

  • Deterministic: stable ordering, canonical digests, explicit states.
  • Evidence-preserving: source paths, provenance, diagnostics, missing evidence.
  • Backend-neutral above adapters: common contracts without erasing native meaning.
  • Bounded: filesystem scope, workers, output, arrays, queries, bodies, queues.
  • Extensible: adapter entry points, rule packs, independent protocol versions.

07

A strong boundary is part of the product.

Engineering AI requires explicit limits. CaeReflex records evidence and consistency checks without pretending to certify engineering truth.

CaeReflex is

  • read-only CAE evidence acquisition;
  • provenance-preserving normalization;
  • spatial and dimensional evidence infrastructure;
  • deterministic consistency checking;
  • governed revision and review workflow;
  • bounded integration infrastructure.

CaeReflex is not

  • a solver, mesher, or CAD repair tool;
  • a convergence or mesh-independence proof;
  • a simulation validation or certification authority;
  • an experimental validation substitute;
  • an autonomous engineering decision-maker;
  • a design-safety approval system.
